FAQs

What services does AmCan offer?

AmCan provides easy-to-approve loans specifically designed to help immigrants in the U.S. and Canada establish credit history.

Who can apply for AmCan's services?

Any newly arrived immigrant in the U.S. or Canada, regardless of their country of origin, can apply for our services.

How quickly can I start building credit with AmCan?

You can start building your credit immediately after your loan application is approved and your repayment plan is in place.

How secure is banking with AmCan?

Your financial security is our top priority. We employ robust security infrastructure and are partnered with Equifax for seamless and secure credit reporting.

How flexible is the repayment plan?

Our repayment plan is designed to be flexible and convenient. Payments commence 30 days post loan approval, and we work with you to establish a plan that suits your needs.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees. You can use our interactive loan calculator to clearly understand your loan amount, associated fees, and payment plan.
